Dr. Nim Game

Instructions

Welcome to Dr. Nim! The goal of the game is to avoid taking the last piece. On each turn, you can take 1, 2, or 3 pieces from a single pile. Dr. Nim will then take its turn. The player who takes the last piece loses the game.

To make a move, enter the pile number and the number of pieces you want to take from that pile, then click "Take". Good luck!
